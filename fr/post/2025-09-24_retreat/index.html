REDEEM retreat in Annecy

Cédric Gouy-Pailler
sept. 20, 2025 2 min. de lecture

We are pleased to announce the REDEEM project retreat taking place in Annecy.
This two-day seminar will bring together project members and partners to present, exchange, and discuss ongoing research on decentralized, robust and privacy-preserving learning.

10 Chemin du Bray
74940 Annecy-le-Vieux
